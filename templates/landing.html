About MRI-Insights

Revolutionizing Medical Imaging

Our Mission

MRI-Insights is a cutting-edge medical imaging platform that leverages artificial intelligence to assist healthcare professionals in detecting brain tumors from MRI scans. Our mission is to make advanced medical imaging technology accessible, accurate, and efficient.

Project Overview

Our final year project, "Development of Automated Supervised Detection System for Medical Images", focuses on creating a machine learning-based tool that assists in the analysis of brain MRI scans. The system aims to provide accurate and timely detection of abnormalities, particularly tumors or lesions, thereby supporting medical professionals in diagnostic decision-making.

How It Works

1

Preprocessing

MRI scans are first preprocessed to normalize and enhance image quality, ensuring optimal input for the AI model.

2

Training

A CNN-based model is trained on labeled datasets of MRI scans, learning to identify patterns associated with brain tumors.

3

Detection

Once trained, the model can detect and classify anomalies like brain tumors with high precision and accuracy.

4

Output

The system highlights detected areas and provides a prediction with confidence levels, aiding medical professionals in diagnosis.
